Our services

What we offer

Choose the right type of support: careworkers for hands-on assistance at home, or nurses for IV therapy and workplace wellness programmes. If you’re unsure, contact us — we’ll point you to the right team.

Careworkers

Home assistance by careworkers

Careworkers support clients in their own homes — help with routine activities, companionship, and practical assistance according to a care plan. This is our core hands-on home service.

  • Scheduled visits tailored to your needs
  • Respectful, consistent support
  • Not nurse-led general nursing care

Nurses

Corporate wellness programmes

Nurse-led workplace wellness — screenings, education, and structured programmes designed with HR and leadership so employees stay informed and supported.

  • Programme design & rollout
  • On-site or hybrid options
  • Reporting for your organisation

Nurses

Home IV therapy

Nurses visit clients at home for IV therapy only — screening, sterile technique, consent, and aftercare. Not for non-IV domestic assistance.

  • Clinical oversight
  • Clear information before treatment
  • Booking for eligible clients

Nurses

Wellness / beauty IV

Nurse-delivered wellness IV options where appropriate — hydration, energy, recovery — with assessment, consent, and guidance you can trust.

  • Client-first assessment
  • Discreet, comfortable visits
  • Clear aftercare guidance

About us

Mwinsi Nkosi Health Care Group

We are a health care group built around Caring4u at home. With Careworkers that provide dependable assistance where clients live. Nurses deliver IV therapy at home, corporate wellness programmes, and wellness IV — keeping clinical work with the right professionals.

Clients choose us for clear communication, dependable scheduling, and teams who treat every engagement with respect. Organisations partner with us for nurse-led wellness programmes and screenings that support healthier, more engaged teams.

  • Patient- and client-centred approach
  • Compliance with applicable health regulations
  • Ongoing training for our care professionals
